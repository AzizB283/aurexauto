# AurexAuto Design Guidelines

## Visual Foundation

**Theme:** Dark premium aesthetic
- Background: Deep charcoal `#0B0F14`
- Cards/Sections: `#141823`
- Primary Accent: Electric Purple `#8A5CFF`
- Secondary Accent: Neon Blue `#3DDCFF`

**Typography:**
- Headlines: Poppins or Space Grotesk (bold, modern)
- Body: Inter or DM Sans
- Code accents: JetBrains Mono (sparingly)
- Text colors: Soft white `#E6E9EF` (main), Gray `#9AA0AE` (secondary)

**Animation:** Minimal, smooth micro-interactions only - scroll fade-ins, subtle glows, gentle parallax. No heavy animations.

**Spacing:** Use Tailwind units of 4, 6, 8, 12, 16, 20 for consistent rhythm

## Layout Structure

**Background:** Dark gradient with animated soft glow, subtle AI pattern (dots/neural mesh)

- Tagline: "Automate. Assist. Accelerate."
- Headline: "Build AI systems that work for your business — 24/7."
- Subtext: "We design AI chatbots, voice assistants, automations, and dashboards that save time, cut costs, and convert more customers."
- Primary CTA: "Get a Free Consultation"
- Secondary CTA: "See Live Demo"
